# Disclaimer

This repository contains **sanitized DFIR case studies and lab writeups** created by the examiner for educational/portfolio purposes.

## Educational Use Only
All content is provided **for learning and professional demonstration**. It is not intended to facilitate wrongdoing, unauthorized access, or misuse of tools.

## No Sensitive or Harmful Materials Included
To protect privacy and ensure responsible disclosure, this repository **does not include**:
- Original evidence images (e.g., disk/memory images), full datasets, or proprietary lab materials
- Malware samples, payloads, exploit code, or weaponized artifacts
- Credentials, personal identifiers, or any data that could be used to identify real individuals

Any potentially identifying information is replaced with placeholders such as **[REDACTED]**.

## Authorization & Ethics
All analyses were performed in controlled, authorized environments (e.g., coursework labs, intentionally vulnerable test systems, or approved exercises). The examiner does not condone illegal activity and supports ethical security practice.

## Accuracy & Limitations
Findings are based on the artifacts available at the time of analysis and may not represent a complete picture. The writeups reflect the examiner’s best effort and professional judgment within the scope of each case.

## AI / Data Handling Note
